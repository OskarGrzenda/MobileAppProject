Types Of Clouds

      High Level Clouds

      cirrocumulus cloud

      cirrus cloud

      ABOUT

      These can be found at

      around 5km-13km above

      ground level.

      WEATHER

      High level clouds are formed

      from ice crystals as they

      are very high up where it is

      cold

          Mid Level Clouds

          altocumulus cloud

          altostratus cloud

          ABOUT

          These can be found at around

          2km-7km above ground level.

          WEATHER

          Mid level clouds mean humidity

          during the summer and can

          mean there will be thunderstorms

          in the evening

              Low Level Clouds

              stratus cloud

              cumulus cloud

              ABOUT

              These can be found at

              around 0km-2km above

              ground level.

              WEATHER

              Low level clouds usually

              result in fog, rain

              or possibly snow
